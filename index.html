Nikola Tesla - Serbian and American inventor

The man who changed the world

More info about Nikola Tesla:

  • 1856 - Born and raised in the Austrian Empire (modern-day Croatia);
  • 1870 - Tesla start studing engineering and physics without receiving a degree;
  • 1884 - He emigrated to the United States, where he became a naturalized citizen;
  • 1885 - Tesla received funding for the Tesla Electric Light Company;
  • 1895 - Tesla designed what was among the first AC hydroelectric power plants at Niagara Falls;
  • 1901 - Tesla began work on the free energy project on a site on Long Island, New York;
  • 1943 - Poor and reclusive, Tesla died of coronary thrombosis in New York City.
